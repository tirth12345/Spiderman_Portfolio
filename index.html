All you need to Know about me

Hey there! I’m your friendly neighborhood Spider-Man, but you can call me Spidey. My real name? Peter Parker. I’m just a regular guy, juggling college life, relationships, and, oh yeah—being a superhero.

How It All Started

One fateful day, during a school field trip to a science lab, a radioactive spider bit me, and boom—everything changed. Suddenly, I had superhuman strength, agility, and the ability to cling to walls. Oh, and web-slinging? That came later, after I invented my web-shooters. Yeah, I’m a bit of a science nerd.

The Powers

I got some pretty cool tricks up my sleeve: Super Strength: I can lift cars, punch through walls, and take down baddies twice my size. Wall-Crawling: I stick to any surface, letting me climb skyscrapers or sneak around unnoticed. Spider-Sense: It's like a sixth sense. I can feel danger before it happens, which helps me dodge attacks. Web-Shooters: These bad boys help me swing from building to building and capture enemies in webbing. I made them myself using my science smarts.

The Villains

From street-level thugs to superpowered foes, I’ve got my hands full. Some of my most notorious enemies include:

  • Green Goblin (Norman Osborn):

    A twisted genius and one of my biggest enemies. He's got gadgets, bombs, and a personal vendetta against me.

  • Doctor Octopus (Otto Octavius): With those mechanical tentacles, Doc Ock is a force to be reckoned witVenom:

    This alien symbiote bonded with Eddie Brock and turned into one of my deadliest foes.

  • The Sinister Six:

    A team of supervillains who gang up to take me down, and trust me, they never make it easy

Some Key Spider-People Across the Multiverse:

  • Peter Parker (The Classic):

    That’s me, your original Spider-Man from Earth-616. But in other universes, things change drastically.

  • Miles Morales:

    In an alternate universe (Earth-1610), Miles takes over the role after his world’s Peter Parker dies. Miles has his own unique powers like invisibility and venom blasts. He's a fresh, young Spider-Man with a big heart.

  • Spider-Gwen (Gwen Stacy):

    In her universe (Earth-65), she got bit by the radioactive spider instead of me. She becomes Spider-Woman, or Spider-Gwen, and fights her own battles while rocking a sleek white-and-pink suit.

  • Spider-Man Noir:

    This is a gritty, 1930s detective version of Spider-Man from a dark, black-and-white universe (Earth-90214). He fights crime in the shadows, solving cases with a darker, more noir style.

    Spider-Ham (Peter Porker):

    In the universe of Earth-8311, Peter Porker is a spider bitten by a radioactive pig. He’s a cartoonish, fun-loving Spider-Ham who fights crime with a sense of humor.

  • Peni Parker & SP//dr:

    In a futuristic anime-like universe (Earth-14512), Peni Parker operates a robotic suit called SP//dr. She’s a teenager with a psychic link to the robot, and she brings a whole new flavor to the Spider-Verse.

  • Spider-Man 2099 (Miguel O'Hara):

    In the future (Earth-928), Miguel O’Hara is a brilliant scientist who ends up with spider-like abilities through a genetic experiment. His suit and tech are way ahead of mine.
